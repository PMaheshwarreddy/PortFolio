HOW I WORK

Three principles
I won't compromise on

OO1

Accuracy over hype

A model that's 80% accurate in a demo and unreliable in production is worse than no model. I benchmark, validate, and ship real numbers — like 95.23% classification accuracy, not best-case screenshots.

OO2

Architecture first

Modular, SOLID, testable code isn't polish — it's what lets an AI workflow survive real users, real data, and real scale. I design for the second version of the system, not just the first demo.

OO3

Ship, measure, iterate

Every project gets deployed — Docker, Cloud Run, CI/CD, monitoring. I'd rather hand over something running in production than a notebook that "works on my machine."
